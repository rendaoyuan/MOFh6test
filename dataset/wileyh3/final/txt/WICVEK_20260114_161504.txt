Synthesis of 3a: An aqueous mixture (10 mL) of Eu2O3, Cu(NO3)2, ip in the ratio of 1:2:1 was placed in a Teflon reactor (23 mL), and heated at 170 °C for 5 d, and then it was cooled to room temperature at 5 °C h−1. Blue crystals were obtained in 53% yield based on Cu.

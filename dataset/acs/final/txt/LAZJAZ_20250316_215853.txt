Synthesis of {[Zn2(TPOM)(5-OH-bdc)2]·(DMF)(H2O)2}n (2). A mixture of Zn(NO3)2·6H2O (29.7 mg, 0.1 mmol), 5-OH-H2bdc (18.2 mg, 0.1 mmol), and TPOM (22.2 mg, 0.05 mmol) was dissolved in 10 mL of DMF/ H2O (1/1, v/v). Colorless block crystals were obtained in 58% yield based on TPOM.

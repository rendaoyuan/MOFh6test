RUFRIV
Chemical_Name: catena-[tris(μ-isophthalato)-bis(μ-hydrogen 2,2',2''-nitrilotriethanolato)-aqua-penta-zinc hydrate]
Number: 1031384
Synonyms: N/A
A mixture of Zn(NO3)2·6H2O (0.3 g, 1 mmol), H3tea (0.3 g, 2 mmol), 1,3-benzenedicarboxylic acid (0.17 g, 1 mmol), and Et3N (0.2 g, 2 mmol) in methanol (20 ml) was sealed in a Teflon-lined stainless steel container and heated at 110ºC for 20 hours, then slowly cooled to room temperature. The resulted colorless crystals were washed with methanol and dried in air. Yield: 34%.

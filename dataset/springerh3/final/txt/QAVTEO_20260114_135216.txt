Synthesis of Complex 1 A mixture containing Ni(NO3)2·6H2O (0.2 mmol), H2DBA (0.1 mmol), BIDPE (0.1 mmol) and NaOH (0.2 mmol) in water/methanol (15 mL, v/v, 2/1) was sealed in a 25 mL Teflon lined stainless steel container and heated at 160 °C for 72 h. Blue block crystals of 1 were washed by water and methanol several times and collected by filtration with a field of 57%.

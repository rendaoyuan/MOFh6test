A mixture of Cu(NO3)2·6H2O (0.5 mmol), KSCN (0.5 mmol), bpy (0.5 mmol) and H2O (10 ml) was mixed in a 23 ml Teﬂon reactor, which was heated at 160 ◦C for 3 days and then cooled to room temperature at a rate of 5 K h−1. Red crystals of 2 were obtained in 78% yield.

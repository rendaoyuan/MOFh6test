Cd(mip)(EtOH) (2) A mixture of Cd(NO3)2·4H2O (0.0926 g, 0.3 mmol), H2mip (0.036 g, 0.2 mmol), triethylamine (0.02 mL), and EtOH (5 mL) in a 23-mL Teflon reactor, which was heated at 110 °C for five days and then cooled to room temperature at a rate of 5 K h−1. Colorless crystals of 2 were obtained (43% yield based on Cd(NO3)2).

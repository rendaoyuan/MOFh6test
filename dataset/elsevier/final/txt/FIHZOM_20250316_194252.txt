Chemical_Name: catena-(tetrakis(μ2-Pyridine-2,6-dicarboxylato-N,O,O',O'')-tetra-aqua-di-copper(ii)-lanthanum(iii) nonahydrate)
Number: 249236
Synonyms: N/A
A mixture of La2O3 (0.082 g, 0.25 mmol), CuO (0.040 g, 0.5 mmol), 2,6-pyridinedicarboxylic acid (0.168 g, 1.0 mmol), and H2O (20 mL) in a mole ratio of about 1:2:4:300 was heated in a 25-mL stainless-steel reactor with a Teflon liner at 140 °C for 80 h. Green–blue crystals of 1 formed. Yield 80%.

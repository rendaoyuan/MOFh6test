Number: 884148
Synonyms: N/A
[Co(4apa)(H2O)] (1) A mixture of Co(NO3)2·6H2O (0.116g, 0.40mmol), 4-aminophthalic acid (0.054g, 0.30mmol), NaOH (0.016g, 0.40mmol) and H2O (10mL) was sealed in a 25mL Teflon-lined stainless steel vessel and heated at 150°C for 2days, and then cooled to room temperature. Red block-shaped crystals were filtered off, washed with water and dried in air. Yield: 67% based on 4apa.

Number: 714523
Synonyms: N/A
Synthesis of [Co(μ-succinato)(H2O)2(pyridine)2] n (1) To 10cm3 of pyridine solution containing 0.059g (0.5mmol) succinic acid was added 10cm3 of a dimethylformamide (DMF) solution of cobalt(II) acetate tetrahydrate, 0.124g (0.5mmol). The mixture was stirred and then allowed to stand for several days at room temperature, after which pink crystals were obtained (yield: 81.7%).

Preparation of complex ZnCo(OH)(pheno)(p-BDC)1.5·H2O (1) A mixture containing Zn(NO3)2·6H2O (0.10 mmol), Co(NO3)2· 6H2O (0.10 mmol), H2BDC (0.20 mmol), pheno (0.10 mmol) and NaOH (0.20 mmol) in water (10 ml) was sealed in a 18 ml Teflon lined stainless steel container and heated at 150 °C for 72 h. Purple block crystals of 1 were washed by deionized water several times and collected by filtration with a field of 73 %.

Preparation of ZnL–DMF: A solution of H3L (0.025m) in DMF (3.0 mL) was mixed with Zn(NO3)2·6H2O in DMF (1.0 mL) in a tightly capped glass vial. The contents were heated at 908C. Dagger-shaped crystals of ZnL–DMF were obtained after 48 h: yield 81% (0.019 g, 0.025 mmol).

Number: 853984
Synonyms: N/A
Preparation of 2 13.3 mg (0.1 mmol) of glutaric acid and 23.7 mg (0.1 mmol) of Cu(NO3)2·H2O were dissolved in 4 mL H2O and carefully layered by a 4 mL acetone solution of 4,4′-trimethylene dipyridine ligand (40.5 mg, 0.2 mmol). Suitable crystals for X-ray analysis were obtained in two weeks. The yield was 22.3 mg (63.6%) for compound 2.
